## Purpose

- **🔷** Decide what **`Tree*`**, **`FileBuffer*`**, **`FileHistory`**, **`Diff/*`** belong in a **thin RPC-wrapper** `libocfiles` vs daemon-only vs other libraries.
- **🔷** Separate step from **`*Files` / `DeleteManager`** cutover — those are list/delete facades; tree/buffer is editor + indexing + diff plumbing.
- **ℹ️** Goal: future **`libocfiles`** is mostly RPC + GTK row types; avoid dragging daemon indexing/tree-sitter into the client by default.

## Questions to answer (🔷)
