# 2.10.4.4 — `ProjectManager` (client and server)

**Status:** PROPOSAL — second delivery.

**Depends on:** [`2.10.4.3`](2.10.4.3-daemon-and-rpcclient.md)

---

## Client — `libocfiles/V2/ProjectManager.vala`

- Namespace **`OLLMfiles`**; do not edit `libocfiles/ProjectManager.vala` in place.
- Construct → **`RpcClient`** → `Daemon.hello`.
- Public methods → **`ProjectManager.{method}`** on wire (same method names as Vala where possible).

**Client-only (stay in V2 PM or UI, no RPC):**

- `active_project`, `active_file` — local pointers; sync from RPC results
- Gtk: re-emit `active_project_changed`, `active_file_changed`, `file_metadata_changed` after successful calls
- `disable_initial_scan` — client flag passed as `params.skip_scan` on activate

---

## Server — `ollmfilesd`

- One **`OLLMfiles.ProjectManager`** (DB, `ProjectList` / `projects`, scan + index queues).
- Dispatch **`ProjectManager.{method}`** to that instance.

---
